## üìù Content SEO Optimization Standards

### On-Page SEO Checklist (Every Page)

#### **Title Tags**
```
Pattern: [Primary Keyword] | [Benefit] | NicheFinders AI

Examples:
- Homepage: "AI Automation for Home Service Businesses | 3-5x ROI in 90 Days | NicheFinders AI"
- Tampa page: "AI Automation Tampa | Business Automation Consultant FL | NicheFinders AI"
- Home Service: "AI Automation for HVAC & Plumbing Companies | NicheFinders AI"
```

**Rules**:
- Max 60 characters
- Keyword at beginning
- Include location when relevant
- Brand at end

#### **Meta Descriptions**
```
Pattern: [Value prop] + [Social proof] + [CTA]. [Unique differentiator].

Examples:
- "Proven AI automation infrastructure for home service companies. 3-5x ROI in 90 days, 15+ years experience. Book free strategy call. We build systems you own, not SaaS subscriptions."

- "Tampa businesses: automate operations with custom AI systems. Voice agents, workflow automation, CRM integration. Local case studies, proven results. Schedule consultation today."
```

**Rules**:
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Clear benefit
- Strong CTA
- Location (if city page)

#### **Header Structure**
```
H1: One per page, primary keyword
  - "AI Automation for Home Service Businesses in Florida"

H2: Secondary keywords, section topics
  - "How AI Voice Agents Transform Home Service Lead Response"
  - "The 6-Stage Customer Journey Automation System"
  - "Case Study: 4.2x ROI for Tampa HVAC Company"

H3: Sub-sections
  - "Stage 1: Lead Generation & Capture"
  - "Automated Multi-Source Lead Collection"
```

#### **Keyword Density**
- Primary keyword: 1.5-2.5% natural density
- First paragraph must contain primary keyword
- First H2 should contain variation of primary keyword
- Conclusion should contain primary keyword
- Use semantic variations throughout

#### **Internal Linking Strategy**
**Minimum 3 internal links per page**:
- Related service pages
- Relevant case studies
- 6-Stage System page (from most pages)
- City pages (cross-link nearby cities)
- Resources/blog content

**Anchor Text Patterns**:
- Exact match: "AI automation for home service businesses"
- Partial match: "learn more about our 6-stage system"
- Branded: "NicheFinders AI proprietary framework"
- Generic + keyword: "see how we help HVAC companies automate"

#### **External Linking (Authority Building)**
Link to authoritative sources:
- NFPA standards (if relevant)
- Industry statistics (Bureau of Labor, IBISWorld)
- Technology partners (OpenAI, Anthropic documentation)
- Local business bureaus, chambers of commerce (city pages)
- CRM platforms (ServiceTitan, Workiz, Housecall Pro)
