The average plumbing company misses 30–40% of inbound calls. Not because they don't want the business — because someone's under a sink or on a roof when the phone rings.

That missed call doesn't wait. It calls the next company on the list.

## The Hidden Revenue Problem

Most [home service contractors](/industries/home-services) think their revenue problem is marketing. It's not. It's capture rate.

If you're generating 100 leads a month and closing 25 of them, getting to 100 leads isn't your bottleneck. Getting to 50 closings from the same 100 leads is.

That's a speed and follow-up problem. AI solves both. [Research from Lead Response Management](https://www.leadresponsemanagement.org) confirms that responding within 60 seconds increases conversion by 391% — a bar no manual process reliably clears.

## What Contractors Are Actually Building

Here's the setup we deploy for home service companies across Florida:

**Missed Call Text-Back**
Every missed call triggers an automated text within 30 seconds: "Hey, this is [Company]. Sorry we missed you — we're on a job right now. What's going on? We'll get back to you shortly."

Response rates: 40–60% of people who would have moved on engage with this text.

**Lead Qualification Bot**
When a new lead comes in through any channel — web form, Google ad, Facebook, referral — an AI bot qualifies them immediately. Gets the job type, urgency, location, and preferred contact time before a human ever touches it.

Your office staff or technicians only talk to qualified leads who are ready to book.

**Job Follow-Up & Review Request**
24 hours after job completion, an automated sequence goes out asking for a [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com) review. Simple, personalized, timed right when satisfaction is highest.

Companies using this consistently generate 3–5x more reviews per month than before.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is a missed call text-back and how does it work?**
A: A missed call text-back automatically detects unanswered inbound calls and sends a text to the caller within 30–60 seconds. It lets them know you received their call and invites a response — keeping the conversation alive before they move to the next contractor on their list. Response rates run 40–60% for calls that would otherwise go cold.

**Q: How much does AI lead response automation cost?**
A: For most home service businesses, the core system — missed call text-back plus lead qualification bot — runs $300–$800 per month. A full stack including follow-up sequences and review automation typically costs $600–$1,500/month depending on integration complexity and call volume.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: The highest-impact components — missed call text-back and lead qualification — go live within 2–3 weeks. That includes messaging approval, integration with your existing phone system and CRM, and testing. Review automation typically gets layered in during week 3–4.

**Q: Does AI lead response work with my CRM and scheduling software?**
A: Yes, in most cases. We integrate with [Jobber](https://getjobber.com), [Housecall Pro](https://www.housecallpro.com), [ServiceTitan](https://www.servicetitan.com), and other major platforms used by home service businesses. Compatibility is confirmed in the first discovery call.

**Q: What's the difference between AI lead response and a traditional answering service?**
A: An answering service takes a message and emails it to you — and you call back hours later, usually after the lead has moved on. AI lead response qualifies the caller immediately, logs the data to your CRM, starts a follow-up sequence, and can even book appointments — all without a human involved. The results are measurably different.
